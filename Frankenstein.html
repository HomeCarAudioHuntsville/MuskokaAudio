The Frankenstein Speaker Project.

Where speakers come to be given New life!

We aim to let no speaker die here!

some amazing speakers end up in the trash for no good reason or because of a simple issue. Here at Home and car Audio we restore speakers that have been left behind or forgotten and turn them into something better then they were before! The most expensive part of a speaker is usually the box and this is where manufactures like to cut corners. I am able to take these cheaply made boxes and apply new glue, Silicone and bracing to make the box more solid then the day it came out of the factory.

Along with that I install new woofers/tweeters if necessary and setup the speakers outside for testing with a microphone. This is when I will start to develop the crossover network for the speakers and confirm the selections by ear and microphone. Once I have finished that I will bring them into my testing room and sit down with them for a few hours playing with the crossover a bit to see what sounds best.

once done I'm left with a set of speakers that have been brought back from the dead! Once someones trash, now ready to be used for another 30 years! These speakers will always offer amazing bang for your buck! I only want a small profit on these as they help keep our landfills smaller!

So if you have a set of speakers that you think are beyond saving, think again! Bring them to me and let's see what we can do together.
